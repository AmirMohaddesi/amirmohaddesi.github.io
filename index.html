About

Let me introduce myself.

I’m an AI/ML engineer with a Ph.D. in Computer Science from UC Irvine (CARL). I build multi-robot systems in ROS2 and integrate LLM planners, memory, and tools for language-guided autonomy.

Recent work & focus

  • ROS2 platform for navigation from human demonstrations (PyTorch), distributed multi-agent simulation, and Webots/Gazebo sim-to-real.
  • Peer-reviewed work on telepresence navigation and cognitive load (IEEE ICDL 2024) and strategy diversity in robot teams (SAB 2024).
  • Now: communication-aware multi-robot coordination with transformers and tool-use.

Outside work: hiking, camping, games, and time with my cat.
